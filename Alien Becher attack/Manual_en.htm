Alien Becher attack

Welcome to game Alien Becher attack! This game is 3D FPS ( First person shooter ) game, or game from the first person view. In this manual, you can find some useful advices to play the game. In this game, there are 5 levels, 5 weapons and 16 objects.
Game was created under OpenGL graphics library. If your game is going very slowly and you've got a good computer, visit www.opengl.org and download drivers for your graphic card.

Controls:

Here are default controls:
Move forward-Arrow up
Move backward-Arrow down
Step left-Arrow left
Step right-Arrow right
Jump-Right mouse button
Shoot-Left mouse button
Use-ENTER
Of course, you can change controls in game options.

System requirements:

Processor:700 Mhz
Memory RAM: 32 MB
Graphic card: 32 MB

Quicksave and quickload:

Very useful thing during playing is using quick save and quick load. Everytime, that you get through some complicated game part, press F6. When you die, press F9 or go to "Load game" menu and load game "Quick.abs".

Weapons:

Pistol:Classic pistol, in which you've got infinite ammo.
Automatic shotgun:Shoots 8 shells at once. It has automatic reloading. You can have up to 80 shells. This weapon is not very good for big distances. You will find it in first level behind first locked door.
UMP submachine gun:One of stronger weapons. Shoots lots of bullets very fast. Your hands shake when shooting, so weapon is a little bit unaccurate. You can have up to 400 bullets. You can find it in first level behind some door.
XP4 dvojitý laser:Second most powerful weapon in the game. 100% exact, so it is also good for big distances. It has two cannons, that changes. You can have up to 300 cells. You will find it in the factory.
K12 raketomet:The most powerful weapon in the game. It uses K12 rockets, that are easy to carry, but very powerful. It's very good to use this weapon for killing lots of enemies. You can have up to 90 rockets. You will find this weapon in the factory, too.
High explosive grenade:Grenades are useful, when lots of enemies are very close to themselves. Upgrading explosion will smash them all. You can have up to 4 grenades.
